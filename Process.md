## Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in the present tense:

1.  I ___ (to be) a student.
2.  She ___ (to have) a cat.
3.  They ___ (to go) to school every day.
4.  He ___ (to play) football on weekends.
5.  We ___ (to study) English.
6.  The sun ___ (to rise) in the east.

---

## Answers

1.  I **am** a student.
2.  She **has** a cat.
3.  They **go** to school every day.
4.  He **plays** football on weekends.
5.  We **study** English.
6.  The sun **rises** in the east.
